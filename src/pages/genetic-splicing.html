Implementation Details

A hybrid rendering experiment:

  • WebGL2: Renders a Double Helix wireframe using `GL_LINES`.
  • WebGPU: Simulates a swarm of "Enzyme" particles that are attracted to the helix radius and spiral along it.
  • Interaction: Mouse interaction triggers a "Splicing Beam" that disrupts and pushes particles.
