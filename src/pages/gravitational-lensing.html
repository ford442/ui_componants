Gravitational Lensing

A hybrid WebGL2 / WebGPU experiment.

WebGL2: Renders a starfield distorted by General Relativity (Gravitational Lensing) around a singularity.

WebGPU: Simulates an accretion disk of 100,000 particles orbiting and spiraling into the event horizon.

WebGPU not supported. Showing WebGL2 only.
