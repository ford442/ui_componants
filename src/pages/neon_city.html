Implementation Details

A hybrid rendering experiment:

  • WebGL2: Renders the infinite scrolling cityscape using instanced rendering for performance.
  • WebGPU: Simulates and renders thousands of "data rain" particles using compute shaders.
  • Interaction: Hover to highlight buildings. Click to trigger a "Cyber-Scan" pulse.
