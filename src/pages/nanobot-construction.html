Implementation Details

A hybrid rendering experiment:

  • WebGL2: Renders the "Blueprint" (Icosahedron Wireframe) using standard line rendering.
  • WebGPU: Simulates 50,000 "Nanobot" particles that seek specific target positions along the blueprint edges.
  • Interaction: Mouse interaction scatters the bots, simulating a disruption field. When released, they autonomously rebuild the structure.
