Synaptic Fire

WebGL2 Membrane WebGPU Neurotransmitters

A microscopic simulation of synaptic transmission. WebGL2 raymarches the organic pre-synaptic and post-synaptic terminals using SDFs. WebGPU simulates thousands of neurotransmitter particles diffusing across the synaptic cleft.

Interaction: Click to trigger Action Potential (Burst). Move mouse to influence electric field.
